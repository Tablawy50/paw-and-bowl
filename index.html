New Recipe

Hearty Beef & Rice
— Vet's Formula

Precise breakdown by our veterinary nutritionist for a complete, balanced beef & rice meal for adult dogs.

🥩Lean Ground Beef55%
High-quality protein, iron, zinc & B12. Use 90% lean to avoid excess saturated fat.
🌾Brown Rice25%
Easily digestible slow-release carb. Rich in manganese and dietary fibre.
🥕Carrots & Peas10%
Beta-carotene, vitamin K, fibre. Supports eyesight & immune function.
🍠Sweet Potato7%
Complex carbs, vitamins A & C, potassium. Gut-friendly & anti-inflammatory.
🥦Broccoli & Spinach3%
Antioxidants, folate & calcium. Keep below 5% to prevent gas.
🐟Salmon Oil+
Omega-3 EPA/DHA for coat, joints & brain. 1 tsp per 500g served.
👨‍⚕️

Vet Nutritionist Note

Use 90%+ lean beef, cook thoroughly, avoid onion/garlic/seasoning. Add eggshell powder for calcium balance — beef is phosphorus-rich but low in calcium, critical for long-term bone health.
